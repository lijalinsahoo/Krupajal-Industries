About Krupajal Industries

KRUPAJAL INDUSTRIES PRIVATE LIMITED is a leading specialist water treatment / purifier machineries / products supplier operating in Maharashtra, Gujarat, West Bengal and Odisha. The company supplies quality, portable and effluent water treatment machineries / products / solutions to municipalities, government sectors and industrial companies, food and farming industries as well as domestic customers in the EAST & WEST region of the Country..


Our Mission

The Company mission is to consistently provide comprehensive range of high quality, sustainable and cost effective water treatment / purifier machineries / products / solutions and serve to our valued customers, in order to satisfy their specific needs, objectives for water services delivery. Our Vision

The Company vision is to be selected by Local Government, Industries and Realtors as the preferred specialist water treatment/purifier machineries / products / solutions supplier company in the Eastern and Western region of India.
